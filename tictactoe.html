Tic Tac Toe

This game is for two players. The 'X' player is yellow and the 'O' player is orange. The goal is to get three boxes in a row (accross, down or diagonally). When you enter your letter into a box and click OK, the box will change to your color. The game ends when one player wins or there is a tie.
