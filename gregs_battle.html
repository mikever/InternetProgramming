Battle the Evil Troll

In this game you will battle the evil troll. You can choose your weapon from the three shown — a set of magic rocks that are a lot stronger and heavier than they look, an extremely sharp sword, or a crossbow and bolt. Unfortunately, you do not know ahead of time which weapon the troll will use. You each begin with 100 points. For each round of the battle, the winner takes 10 points from the loser. When either of you reaches 200 points, the battle is over and one of you will lie dead. The winner is determined by the list shown below. Push the button when you are ready to begin the battle, and good luck!
